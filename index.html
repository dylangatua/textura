WHAT IS TEXTURA?

Textura is a product design lab at the forefront of haircare innovation. We’re a Black-owned startup committed to making inventive hair tools for the textured hair community.

Any hair type that is coily, curly, or wavy is considered textured. Around 60% of people on Earth have some form of textured hair, yet, most hair tools are designed with only straight haired users in mind. Too often people with varying hair textures, styles, and hair needs have been neglected.

We use afro-centric design thinking and community-based co–creation to collaboratively build products that resonate with textured hair consumers and empower innovation in their haircare experiences.

THE HYDROPICK IN ACTION

Introducing the HydroPick - Textura's revolutionary hair tool that combines hair styling with hydration. This innovative afro pick features a built in cartridge system and specialized applicating teeth, delivering precise product distribution while you style. Transform your hair care routine with one simple tool that volumizes, moisturizes, and grooms your hair with reliable precision.

Plus, the HydroPick is an accessory. Gone are the days of using boring afro-picks. The HydroPick has a built in charm system that allows you to decorate the handle however you see fit.

THE HYDROPICK IS FOR ALL

Although the HydroPick is made for afro-style hair, it caters to all hair types and styles.

The HydroPick is great for scalp oiling & mositurizing, no matter the hair style.

The HydroPick is great for volumizing hair, whether it be for the scalp or face.
